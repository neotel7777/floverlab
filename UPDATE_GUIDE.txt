+---------------------------+
| How to update FleetCart?  |
+---------------------------+

Following these steps, you can easily update FleetCart to the latest version:

[Important] 1. Please backup your website to mitigate the risk of data loss and facilitate future recovery.

            2. Delete all the files and folders except the following:
                » Files: .env
                » Folders: public, storage

            3. Paste all the files and folders from the new version except the following:
                » Files: .env

You're done! Congratulations on updating your application successfully.
